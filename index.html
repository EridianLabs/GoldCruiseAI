Strategy Overview

GoldCruiseAI uses a multi-layered filtering approach:

  • Trend Analysis: Fast EMA (21), Slow EMA (50), Trend EMA (200)
  • Momentum Confirmation: RSI, CCI, Momentum indicators
  • Higher Timeframe Filter: Optional H4 trend confirmation
  • Volatility Assessment: ATR-based stop loss and take profit calculation
  • Confidence Scoring: Weighted evaluation of indicator agreement, signal strength, volatility, and trend alignment

Trades are only executed when multiple conditions meet configured thresholds. All parameters are adjustable.
